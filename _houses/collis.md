##### Border Collie (Ranger) — House Collis
:
Ability Scores: +2 Wisdom, +1 Dexterity
:
Herding Instinct: As a bonus action, you may mark a creature within 60 feet that you can see for 1 minute. While marked, you have advantage on Survival checks to track it and deal damage ueqal to your proficiency bonus to it with weapon attacks.
:
Lore: Border Collies serve as scouts, wardens, and trailmasters along Canisgard’s borders. Their Houses coordinate patrols, migrations, and long-distance communications between settlements.
